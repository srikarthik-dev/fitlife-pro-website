# Account Deletion Policy

## Delete Your Account

Users can delete their account directly within the application.

Path:

Profile → Settings → Delete Account

## What Happens After Deletion

When an account is deleted:

- Authentication credentials are removed
- User profile information is removed
- Workout records may be removed
- Nutrition records may be removed
- Notifications may be removed

Certain records may be retained temporarily for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance purposes.
